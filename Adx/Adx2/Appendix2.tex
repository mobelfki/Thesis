\section{Background rejection for CNN Tight and Loose WPs}
\label{Adx2:BkgRej}
The background rejections of the CNN Tight and Loose WPs are shown in Figure \ref{BkgRej}. Note that the two WPs are optimized to deliver similar background rejection as the cut-based in bin of $|\eta|$. The optimization is done in the $Z\rightarrow ll\gamma$ events, while figures shows the background rejection in the Z+jets sample. This serves as an additional validation of the CNN performance. 

\begin{figure}[htbp]
    \centering
	\subfloat[][]{\includegraphics[width=.35\textwidth]{Adx/Adx2/Img/BackRej_ETA_UnConverted_Loose_E_Loose_E_CNN_Iso_tight.png}}
	\subfloat[][]{\includegraphics[width=.35\textwidth]{Adx/Adx2/Img/BackRej_ETA_UnConverted_Tight_E_inc_Tight_E_CNN_Iso_tight.png}} \\
	\subfloat[][]{\includegraphics[width=.35\textwidth]{Adx/Adx2/Img/BackRej_ETA_Converted_Loose_E_Loose_E_CNN_Iso_tight.png}}
	\subfloat[][]{\includegraphics[width=.35\textwidth]{Adx/Adx2/Img/BackRej_ETA_Converted_Tight_E_inc_Tight_E_CNN_Iso_tight.png}}
	\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=black!5!white,colframe=white!75!black]
    \caption{Background rejection of the CNN tight (right) and Loose (left) WPs. For unconverted (top) and converted (bottom) photons in bin of $|\eta|$. The rejection is evaluated in the Z+jets sample. The red line shows the rejection for CNN and blue line shows the cut-based rejection.}
    \label{BkgRej}
    \end{tcolorbox}
    
\end{figure}
